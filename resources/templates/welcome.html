Organizations we partner with

WCV began with one individuals desire to bring cardiac care and medical resources to places in the world that lack these means. This organizations mission is to improve cardiac care in individuals with easily treatable diseases. Together we can make a difference and help save lives.

Online and offline training

Each year, WCV takes a small group of health professionals in underdeveloped countries through a learning program. This 10 month course course offers students the training they need to provide improved cardiac care in their communities. Click the button below to read about our experience in Ethiopia.

Improve access to resources

We work with our partners to provide hospitals with medical equipment, reference materials, and other essential supplies.
