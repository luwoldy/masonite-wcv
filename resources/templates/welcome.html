Organizations we partner with

WCV brings cardiac care and medical resources to developing countries. Our mission is to improve care for individuals with easily treatable heart diseases. We do this by training local cardiac care professionals in advance techniques. We also provide local hospitals with essential equipment and resources from our partners.

Online and offline training

Each year, WCV takes a small group of health professionals in underdeveloped countries through a learning program. This 10 month course offers students the training they need to provide improved cardiac care in their communities. Click the button below to read about our experience in Ethiopia.
